To enable readable stack traces in your Sentry errors, you need to upload your source maps to [Sentry](https://sentry.io/welcome/).

The easiest way to configure uploading source maps is by using the [Sentry Wizard](/platforms/javascript/sourcemaps/#uploading-source-maps).
