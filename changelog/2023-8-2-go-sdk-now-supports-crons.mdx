  Our Go SDK now supports Sentry Cron Monitoring. You can now start monitoring your recurring jobs in minutes through the SDK. Update your SDK to [v0.23.0](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-go/releases/tag/v0.23.0), and read our [setup guide](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/go/crons/) to get started.
