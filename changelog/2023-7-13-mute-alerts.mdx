  You can now mute issue alerts and metric alerts. Click the mute button in an email or in the alert details page to mute an alert just for yourself or for everyone. You can unmute them anytime in the future. See the [muting alerts docs](https://docs.sentry.io/product/alerts/#muting-alerts) for more detailed information.
