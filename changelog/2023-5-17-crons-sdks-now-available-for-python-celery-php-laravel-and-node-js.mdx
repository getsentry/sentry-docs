  You can now monitor your recurring jobs in less than five minutes on your favorite platform with these new versions of Sentry's SDKs:

* **Python:** Update your SDK to [v1.22.2](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-python/releases/tag/1.22.2), or read our [setup guide](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/python/crons/) to get started.

* **Celery:** Update your SDK to [v1.22.2](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-python/releases/tag/1.22.2), or read our [setup guide](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/python/guides/celery/crons/) to get started.

* **PHP:** Update your SDK to [v3.16.0](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-php/releases/tag/3.16.0), or read our [setup guide](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/php/crons/) to get started.

* **Laravel:** Update your SDK to [v3.3.3](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-laravel/releases/tag/3.3.3), or read our [setup guide](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/php/guides/laravel/crons/) to get started.

* **Node.JS:** Update your SDK to [v7.51.0](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-javascript/releases/tag/7.51.0), or read our [setup guide](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/node/crons/) to get started.
