  We are proud to announce support for Electron Renderers (UIs)! Profiling offers code-level visibility into your running app, allowing you to identify hot paths and potential bottlenecks in your Electron app, when navigating between different tabs and loading the initial UI.\
\
\
See our Electron profiling [documentation](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/electron/profiling/) to learn more.
