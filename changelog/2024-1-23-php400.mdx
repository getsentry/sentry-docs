We are excited to announce the immediate availability of our PHP SDK v4.4.0. The following improvements are included with this release

Ability to wrap a callback into monitor check-ins. `withMonitor()`

Add new `failure_issue_threshold` and `recovery_threshold` configuration to `MonitorConfig`

See our SDK [changelog](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-php/releases/tag/4.4.0) for more info, and our product docs for Crons here!
