  The Sentry Remix SDK now officially supports Remix v2, with our [7.70.0 release](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry-javascript/releases/tag/7.70.0)! Since the announcement of Remix V2 stable release, we are happy to confirm our SDK will continue helping devs smash bugs and monitor vitals for their Remix applications.

See [our Remix docs for more details](https://docs.sentry.io/platforms/javascript/guides/remix/).
