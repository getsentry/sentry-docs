  Spike Protection notifications are turned off by default to avoid adding more notification noise for users. You can add notification actions to get notified about spikes for particular projects. You can send these notifications to a Slack channel or Pagerduty. Read our [docs](https://docs.sentry.io/product/accounts/quotas/spike-protection/#how-to-detect) to learn more
