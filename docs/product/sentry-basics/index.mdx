**→ [Sign up for Sentry here](https://sentry.io/signup/)**

## How to Get the Most Out of Sentry

Here's what we recommend to get the most out of Sentry:

- **Connect your codebase** by setting up [source code management integrations](/integrations/source-code-mgmt/) and [source maps](/product/sentry-basics/integrate-frontend/upload-source-maps/) (if applicable). This enables readable stack traces, [suspect commits](/product/issues/suspect-commits/), and AI-powered debugging with [Seer](/product/ai-in-sentry/seer/).
- **Set up [alerts](/product/monitors-and-alerts/alerts/)** to know when things you care about go wrong. Get alerted via [Slack](/integrations/notification-incidents/slack/) or [Discord](/integrations/notification-incidents/discord/). See [alert best practices](/product/monitors-and-alerts/alerts/#alerts-best-practices) to get only relevant notifications.
- **Turn on more features** to get deeper context when debugging. Enable [Logs](/product/logs/) to see structured log data alongside your errors, use [Traces & Spans](/product/trace-explorer/) to follow requests across services, and watch [Session Replay](/product/session-replay/) recordings to see exactly what the user experienced.
- **Explore your data** with [Dashboards](/product/dashboards/) for at-a-glance health views and [Application Metrics](/product/metrics/) to track custom counters and gauges.

For practical, hands-on guidance on getting more value from each feature, see our **[Guides](/guides/)**.
