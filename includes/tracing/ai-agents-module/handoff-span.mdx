A span that describes the handoff from one agent to another.

- The spans `op` (transaction mode) or the span's `sentry.op` attribute (stream mode) MUST be `"gen_ai.handoff"`.
- The spans `name` SHOULD be `"handoff from {from_agent} to {to_agent}"`.
- All [Common Span Attributes](#common-span-attributes) SHOULD be set.
